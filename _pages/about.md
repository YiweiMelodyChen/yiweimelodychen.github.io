I am a second year master majoring computer science at [Xi'an Jiaotong University](http://en.xjtu.edu.cn/), working as a research assistant at Shaanxi Provincial Key Laboratory of Big Data Knowledge Engineering since Jan,  2021.

I am now working on Scene Graph Generation, Image-Text Matching and Few-Shot Learning tasks. My research interest includes Computer Vision, Vision&Language and  Logic Reasoning. The goal of my research is to embody AI systems causal reasoning abilities, with greater interpretability.

I graduated from [Special Class for Gifted Young](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special_Class_for_the_Gifted_Young#Xi'an_Jiaotong_University), [Qian Xuesen Honors College](https://bjb.xjtu.edu.cn/), Xi'an Jiaotong University  with a Bachelor's degree. 
At present, I am pursuing Master degree in Computer Science and Technology, advised by [Jun Liu](https://gr.xjtu.edu.cn/en/web/liukeen/4) and [Lingling Zhang](https://gr.xjtu.edu.cn/en/web/zhanglling), will graduate in June, 2024.
